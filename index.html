Event Venue

Event venue location info and gallery

IFEMA Madrid, Spain

IFEMA, whose official name is IFEMA MADRID is a consortium that organizes fairs, shows and congresses in its fairgrounds and Congress Palace located in the district of Barajas, northeast of the city of Madrid, Spain.

F.A.Q

  • Are there any discounts for groups or students?

    Yes, in case of a group of 4 or more a 20% discount will be applied. In case of being a student or having some type of young card, a discount of up to 40% will be applied.

  • Is the event suitable for all ages?

    You must be over 16 years old to access the entire event without accompaniment. For the little ones there will be activities that will not be available although they can access normally.

  • Are there parking spaces available near the venue?

    Parking spaces will be enabled in the surroundings of the venue only for the people of the event.

  • Are pets allowed at the event?

    Pets are not allowed during the event.

  • Is there a set dress code?

    Yes, you must wear a Victorian Era costume and/or steampunk according to the event. (This step is optional, but it helps immerse the assistants into roleplay).

  • Can I bring my camera or recorder to the event?

    Yes, all recordings and photos will be allowed as long as the scenes are not fully recorded.
